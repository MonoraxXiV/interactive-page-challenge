Archery Fair

Welcome to the Archery Fair, our yearly medieval archery event!
We are happy to celebrate with you our 25th anniversary. Expect a day full of excitement, history and of course : precision!

Summary

Archery Fair is held by the Quiver.
Our event will hold two types of competitions: Field archery and the 900 round.
For the kids there will be an initiation to archery with skilled instructors.*
During the day there will be performances by professional archers.
On the premises there will be vendors with gear, traditional food and drinks.
*kids younger than 6 cannot enter the initiations workshop.

Pratical Informations

  • Where : Kalmthoutse Heide, meet up at the Vroente: Putsesteenweg 129 – B-2920 Kalmthout
  • What time: from 9 am to 6 pm.
  • Tickets: Registration will be at the main office until 10 am.
  • For entry tickets prices please refer to the Tickets tab.

Competition Information

Classes to compete in :

900 round :
30 arrows at the distances 60, 50, 40 meters for both men and women. The 122 cm face is used for all distances.

Time of each competition event:

900 round: starts at 10:30 am at the shooting range.
Field archery will be held from 3pm until 6pm in the marked zone.

Bring your own bow for the competitions

How to sign up : Registration will be at the main office until 10 am.
